Kim Jung Gi

An Influential Comic Artist

Here's a timeline of artist Kim Jung Gi's Life:

  • 1975- Born in Goyang, a suburb of Seoul

  • 1994- Entered Dong-Eui University in Busan to pursue a major in arts and design

  • 2001- Chose "Kim Jung Gi" as his "English name" over his legal name, Kim Jung Ki, because "Gi" was "more visually appealing

  • 2011- At the Comic Con in Bucheon and Kim began to draw, Hyun Jin Kim, the CEO of Superani,filmed Kim at work. The video went viral.From then on, invitations to draw live and demand for his expertise skyrocketed.

  • 2022- Died on October 3rd due to a sudden heart attack.
